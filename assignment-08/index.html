How to: Candle Art

Materials needed:

  • wax paper
  • tissue paper
  • regular print paper
  • candle
  • hair dryer
  • scissors
  • tape
Directions
Step 1 On tissue paper, draw a design that will fit on your candle.
Step 2 Draw your design with colored pencils or makers.
Step 3 Cut our your design.
Step 4 Cut out a piece of wax paper to wrap around your candle.
Step 5 Put your design on the candle. Wrap the wax paper tightly around it and tape it in place.
Step 6 Heat your design with a hair dryer. The design will look wet and bright.
Step 7 Once done, carefully remove the wax paper from the candle.
